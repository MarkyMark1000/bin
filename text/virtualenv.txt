Virtualenvwrapper
=================

  mkvirtualenv new_env                  [make a new virtual environment]

  which pip                             [location of virtual env]

  deactivate                            

  workon virtual_env                    [use this virtual environment]

  workon                                [list all virtual environments]

  rmvirtualenv virtual_env              [remove virtual environment]

  [create a virtual environment with a specific python version]
  mkvirtualenv -p ....python3.9 myproj
